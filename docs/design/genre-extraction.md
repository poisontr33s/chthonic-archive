### 7.2 THE FORMULA

```
CAPACITY = (1 - WHR) × CUP_MODIFIER × TIER_MULTIPLIER

Where:
  WHR = Waist-Hip Ratio (lower = more capacity)
  CUP_MODIFIER = K=1.0, J=0.95, I=0.90, H=0.85, G=0.80, F=0.75, E=0.70
  TIER_MULTIPLIER = 0.5=2.0, 1=1.5, 2=1.0, 3=0.75, 4=0.5

Example (The Decorator):
  CAPACITY = (1 - 0.464) × 1.0 × 2.0
           = 0.536 × 1.0 × 2.0
           = 1.072 (normalized to 100 = ~107 capacity points)

Example (Lysandra Thorne):
  CAPACITY = (1 - 0.58) × 0.70 × 1.5
           = 0.42 × 0.70 × 1.5
           = 0.441 (normalized to 100 = ~44 capacity points)
```
